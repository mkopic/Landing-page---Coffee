Sorts

Arabica coffee comes from the beans of a Coffea arabica plant, which originated in Ethiopia.

Robusta Coffee is coffee made from the beans of the Coffea canephora plant, the origins of which are in Africa.

Other species of coffee exist and contain unique varieties (Barako coffee, a Liberica variety from Philippines).
